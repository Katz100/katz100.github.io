iOS And Android

Overview

iOS and Android are the two dominant mobile operating systems in the world. Together, they power billions of smartphones and tablets. Each platform provides its own development tools, programming languages, design standards, and distribution models.

What is iOS?

iOS is Apple’s proprietary mobile operating system used on devices such as:

  • iPhone
  • iPad
  • iPod Touch

Apple tightly controls both hardware and software, creating a highly optimized and secure ecosystem.

What is Android?

Android is an open-source mobile operating system developed by Google. It runs on devices manufactured by companies such as:

  • Samsung
  • Google (Pixel)
  • OnePlus
  • Motorola

Android supports a wide range of hardware configurations.
